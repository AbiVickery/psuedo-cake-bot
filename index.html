Ingredients

  • Retrieve red food dye.
  • Retrieve chocolate cake mix.
  • Retrieve stick of butter.
  • Retrieve 3 raw eggs.
  • Retrieve vanilla liquid flavoring.
  • Retrieve almond oil.
  • Retrieve table-spoon.
  • Retrieve tea-spoon.
  • Retrieve cake pan.

Before mixing,

  • Set oven to bake at 450 degrees farenheit.
  • Get out large mixing bowl.
  • Get out whisk for mixing ingredients.
  • Set out the cake pan.
  • Spray non-stick butter spray on the cake pan.

to mix:

  • Gently crack egg shells and pour egg inards into bowl.
  • Gently add half the stick of butter to the bowl.
  • Gently pour in chocolate cake mix into the bowl.
  • Gently measure 1 tea-spoon of almond oil into the bowl.
  • Gently measure 1 table-spoon of clean water into the bowl.
  • Gently measure 1 tea-spoon of vanilla liquid flavoring into the bowl.
  • Gently add 3 drops of red food dye into the bowl.
  1. Take whisk in hand and Gently mix ingredients in bowl using the whisk in a circular motion.
  2. Keep Gently mixing ingredients until forming a soft mush of one color: the color red.
  3. Take out the cake pan of choice and put it next to the bowl.
  4. Take bowl of red mix and Gently pour red mix into the cake pan of choice.
  5. Smooth out the top layer of mix in the cake pan.
  6. Gently take cake pan over to oven.

to bake:

  1. Open oven door.
  2. Gently set cake pan in oven on rack.
  3. Close oven door.
  4. Set oven timer for 12 minutes.
  5. Wait for 12 minutes.

When timer goes off:

  1. Turn off timer.
  2. Open oven door.
  3. Gently remove cake pan from oven.
  4. Gently set cake pan on cooling rack.
  5. Wait 2 minutes for cake to cool down.

After waiting for cool-down:

  1. Gently remove cake from pan.
  2. Gently place cake onto plate.
  3. Now activate and apply "frosting algorithum".
  1. Gently set cake in spot-light for all to gaze upon.
  2. Step back and look on in awe.

Done!
